ABOUT US

RAFUNI (Pty) Ltd is a 100% black owned enterprise involved in construction, engineering services, distribution, and supply. We are a responsible corporate company committed to job creation across South Africa.

The company's sole director is Miss Netshilindi Lufuno, a black, ambitious and driven young professional woman. She holds a BSC degree in Environmental & Engineering Geology, as well as honours in engineering geology. She is in the process of obtaining two certificates, one in Occupational safety and health and another in project management. The director holds 100% interest in the business.

To be the service provider of choice, to be known and respected for our ability to always deliver exceptional value through service excellence.

Our mission is to provide the best services to our customers.

WHAT WE DO

OUR STRATEGY

RAFUNI's focus on growth is evident in the strategic objectives entrenched in daily business practices, including:

Optimising profitability of established businesses through active enhancement of business efficiencies and strengthening of margins.

Improving market positioning by continually identifying opportunities to further strengthen the group's position as a market leader across the continent.

Organic expansion through penetration of new markets, leveraging the company's client base.

Growth through strategic acquisitions which will enhance the current service offering, deepen and expand existing client relationships and provide healthy return on investment.

Expansion into the rest of the country by leveraging the company's well-established, recognised brands and client expansion, and establishing separate, decentralised businesses with select local partners in the target provinces.
